Quick FAQ

How often do rates refresh?

Rates can be refreshed from the configured backend price source when the proxy is enabled.

Why do purity prices differ?

24K reflects near-pure gold, while 22K and 18K include alloy composition and trade at lower per-gram values.

Can this connect to live API data?

Yes. The page is already wired to the existing `api/gold-price.php` endpoint and falls back to local demo values when unavailable.
